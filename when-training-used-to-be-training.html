When Training used to be Training

Mamawatoto

We finally attended our training as Art at Heart.We arrived one hour late but were still among the earliest.We found the lady trainer distressed making phone calls to her senior counter-part who had organized the training for her.'There is a big group on the way' she was comforted.We tried to encourage the trainer to take it easy and begin the introductions so we can familiarise with each other and flow but she seemed to follow a fixed program where we had to wait until we all  , Slowly and reluctantly people appeared one by one.Two women came with their children it was interesting,

Ok let me get to my main point.What happened to the good old trainings,A financial training with ILO trained trainers;sponsored by mastercard and equity foundation with no stationery for participants,isnt that poor and disorganized?

The lady trainer wearing super tight suit with pants showing off all her goods stands up to introduce the training

"I don't know the time allocated for this training,I don't know the number of people am expecting,I dont know I don't know ...about four times.

What happened to the good old sponsored trainings.First as a trainer you would be taken to a five star hotel for a serious two weeks training
