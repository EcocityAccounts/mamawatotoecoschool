Eco Products

Mamawatoto

Eco handbags

We make eco-handbags using waste print film paper.The bags are not only creatively designed but also quite durable.

Eco sandals

These are sandals created from waste car tyres which if burned cause alot of environmental polution and health risks.

Eco toys

These are toys made out of waste materials like cans, jerricans, bottletops and lids.

The process of making the eco-products is not complicated and can be taught within a couple of hours.

The goal of Mamawtoto's eco-crafts trainings is to enhance environmetal conservation and create employment to women and young people.  Our work is dedicated to helping the Kenyan government to achieve its vision 2030 and the 17 UN millenium development goals.

Mamawatoto
